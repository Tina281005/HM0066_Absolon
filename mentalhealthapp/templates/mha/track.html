Generalized Anxiety Disorder-7 (GAD-7) Test

The Generalized Anxiety Disorder-7 (GAD-7) is a 7-item self-report questionnaire designed to screen and assess the severity of generalized anxiety disorder (GAD). It is widely used in clinical and research settings to measure anxiety symptoms over the past two weeks.
